A Plant's Journey to You

Local, sustainable, organic farmed foods get to you.

1

Plants Grown Local

Roots sources all foods at our restaurant directly from farmers in Chester County, PA. Vegetables, fruits, soy, and rice are grown just a 30-minute truck ride from our restaurant so you know our ingredients are fresh.

2

Environmentally Friendly

We support farmers who are a part of the Grassland Bird Collaboration, ensuring that grassland species thrive while producing high-quality, fresh goods. Monitoring breeding success and fostering healthy ecosystems is integral to their process.

3

Prepared Here at Roots

Once ingredients arrive from local farms to the kitchen, we start preparing food the day you get here to ensure freshness.

4

After Eating

We never waste food. Leftovers go straight into our compost bin, enriching the soil for our personal garden and supporting sustainable agriculture.
