About

Our Journey and Mission

From Passion to Purpose

Founded in 2024 by two passionate culinary artists, Roots is an inclusive vegetarian restaurant dedicated to all dietary preferences. We strive to support sustainable food production while offering delicious meals for everyone.

The concept of Roots sprouted from personal experiences and a desire to create a space where dietary needs are not just accommodated but celebrated.
